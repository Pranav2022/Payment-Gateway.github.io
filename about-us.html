About us

“The best way to find yourself is to loose yourself in the service of others.”

Charity Donation

Our Organization was set up in order to tackle the problems of poverty in Mumbai, especially amongst young children. From its humble origins as a family institution it has grown rapidly, and now its projects cover a wide range of activities located in both India and other developing countries, including Afghanistan, Sudan, Kenya and Burundi.
We are committed to the welfare of people from the most underprivileged strata of society without distinction of caste, creed or color. Its emphasis is on assisting the children and youth who are the citizens of tomorrow and the disabled who need a support to help them join mainstream society.

We provide emergency assistance including mass and mobile feeding, temporary shelter, counseling, search and rescue, medical assistance, and resource distribution.

Vission & Mission

our vision for the future – a future in which each and every child can look forward to a new day, a day free of hunger, suffering, abuse and poverty.

Our mission is to reach every people who need help
